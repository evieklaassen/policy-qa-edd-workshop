# Parental Leave Policy
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Document Owner:** People Operations  
**Policy ID:** HR-LEAVE-002

---

## 1. Overview

Acme Corp provides paid parental leave to eligible employees welcoming a new child through birth, adoption, or foster placement. This policy is designed to meet or exceed statutory requirements in all regions where Acme Corp operates.

---

## 2. Eligibility

- Employees must have completed **90 days of continuous employment** prior to the start of leave.
- Both primary caregivers and secondary caregivers are eligible.
- Policy applies to full-time employees. Part-time employees (20+ hours/week) receive prorated leave pay.
- Contractors and temporary workers are not eligible.

---

## 3. Leave Entitlements by Region

### United States

| Caregiver Role | Leave Entitlement | Pay |
|---------------|------------------|-----|
| Primary caregiver (birth parent) | 16 weeks | 100% base salary |
| Primary caregiver (non-birth, adoption, foster) | 16 weeks | 100% base salary |
| Secondary caregiver | 6 weeks | 100% base salary |

- Acme Corp's paid leave **runs concurrently** with FMLA (12 weeks) where applicable. Employees are not entitled to FMLA leave after exhausting Acme's paid leave.
- California employees: Acme's pay supplement is coordinated with California SDI/PFL. Employees receive the difference between SDI/PFL benefit and 100% base salary from Acme.
- Employees may append accrued PTO to the end of parental leave but are not required to.

### Netherlands

| Caregiver Role | Statutory Entitlement | Acme Supplement |
|---------------|----------------------|-----------------|
| Birth parent (kraamverlof) | 1 week at 100% | N/A |
| Birth parent (bevallingsverlof) | 16 weeks | 100% for full 16 weeks (statutory pays ~70%; Acme tops up to 100%) |
| Partner (geboorteverlof) | 1 week at 100% pay + 5 weeks at 70% | Acme tops up to 100% for all 6 weeks |
| Adoptive/foster parent | 6 weeks paid | 100% for 6 weeks |
| Parental leave (ouderschapsverlof) | 26 weeks (9 weeks at 70% UWV) | Acme does not supplement the 70% UWV portion |

### United Kingdom

| Caregiver Role | Leave Entitlement | Pay |
|---------------|------------------|-----|
| Primary caregiver | 26 weeks enhanced, then up to 26 weeks additional | Weeks 1–16: 100% base salary; Weeks 17–26: statutory (£184.03/week or 90% of average weekly earnings, whichever is lower); After week 26: unpaid |
| Secondary caregiver (paternity) | 2 weeks | 100% base salary (statutory: £184.03/week; Acme tops up) |
| Shared Parental Leave | Up to 50 weeks combined | Pay follows primary caregiver schedule for weeks taken |

### Germany

| Caregiver Role | Leave Entitlement | Pay |
|---------------|------------------|-----|
| Either parent (Elternzeit) | Up to 3 years total (per parent) | Elterngeld (government benefit): 65% of net income, capped at €1,800/month |
| Acme supplement | Applicable for first 14 weeks only | Acme tops up Elterngeld to 100% of base salary for the first 14 weeks; no supplement after |
| Mutterschutz (birth parent only) | 6 weeks before + 8 weeks after birth | 100% paid (statutory + Acme supplement) |

---

## 4. How to Request Parental Leave

1. Notify your manager and HR Business Partner as soon as practicable (at least 8 weeks before intended start date is strongly preferred).
2. Submit a formal leave request in Workday under **Time Off > Leave of Absence > Parental Leave**.
3. HR will send a leave confirmation letter within 5 business days.
4. For US employees, HR will also initiate FMLA paperwork if applicable.

---

## 5. Benefits During Leave

- Health insurance, dental, and vision coverage continue during paid parental leave.
- 401(k) / pension contributions: Company matching continues on the same schedule during fully paid leave. Matching pauses during any unpaid portion.
- Equity vesting continues uninterrupted during parental leave regardless of pay status.
- PTO continues to accrue during paid parental leave.

---

## 6. Return to Work

- Employees returning from parental leave have the right to return to the same or an equivalent role.
- A phased return (e.g., starting at 60% schedule) may be arranged by mutual agreement with the manager and HR, for up to 4 weeks after returning.
- Employees must notify HR of their confirmed return date at least 2 weeks before returning.

---

## 7. Interaction with Other Policies

- Parental leave is **separate from** and does not reduce PTO balances (HR-PTO-001).
- Employees on parental leave are not subject to performance improvement plans initiated during their leave.
